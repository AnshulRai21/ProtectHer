For the Safety of Women and Children

Women's safety analytics involves the use of data and machine learning algorithms to identify patterns and trends related to women's safety. This can include analyzing social media data to detect and prevent online harassment, as well as using IoT-enabled machine learning strategies to create safety management systems. Additionally, women's safety analytics can involve the use of spatial analysis to identify areas with high crime rates and develop targeted interventions. Overall, women's safety analytics aims to provide insights that can inform policies and programs to improve women's safety.
