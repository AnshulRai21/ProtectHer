Our Achievements

At ProtectHer, we take pride in our accomplishments in advancing women's and children's safety. Our achievements include the development of innovative safety analytics tools, successful partnerships with community organizations, and impactful awareness campaigns. We have received recognition for our contributions to safety technology and have made significant strides in improving safety protocols and interventions.
